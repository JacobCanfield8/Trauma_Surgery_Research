RTcotomy ML is a cutting-edge tool designed to assist medical professionals in predicting the outcomes of resuscitative thoracotomy for trauma and critical care patients. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, our tool provides accurate predictions based on comprehensive data.

Our mission is to improve patient outcomes by providing reliable predictions that help inform clinical decisions. We believe that access to accurate, data-driven predictions can make a significant difference in trauma care.

Learn more about our work and the technology behind RTcotomy ML by exploring our tool and resources. If you have any questions or need more information, please don't hesitate to reach out to us through our Contact page.
